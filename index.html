I am a CS Phd at Georgia Tech working with Prof.Sehoon Ha. I am working on cross-embodiment skill transfer, aiming to enable robots with arbitrary morphology to learn skills from human demonstrations. My current focus areas are:

  • Diverse Agile Motion Generation
  • Whole-body Dynamic Manipulation
  • Human-Robot and Robot-Robot Interaction
Feel free to reach out if you are interested in my research.

There are a few folks that own the same name, such as Tianyu Li@MIT and Tianyu Li@UPENN, please make sure you contact to correct Tianyu Li.
